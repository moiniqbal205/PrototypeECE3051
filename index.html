Electrical Energy Systems

ECE3051 teaches the foundations for Power Engineering. Students will be taught about high power electrical equipment that are commonplace in Power Engineering industry.

This unit can potentially guide you to a wide variety of engineering sub-disciplines such as:

High Voltage Power Electronic Applications Smart Grids

Why is Power Engineering Important?

Among its vast range of applications, some notable ones include:

  • Transformers : transfers electrical energy between circuits through induction, and can be used to step up or step down voltage.

  • Power Transmission & Distribution : delivering power from power generation plants to required areas through transmission & distribution lines; used in conjunction with transformers.

  • Renewable Energy : an ever-growing field that is to become an inevitable part of modern & future societies, e.g. solar panels, wind turbines, electric cars, etc.
